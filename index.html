§ I Overview

Starting from a fictional Phase 1 protocol, the pipeline wrote the SAP, DMP, and consent form, simulated the EDC data, built the analysis datasets and tables, and ran a separate QC in R. The tabs open the files, show the data, list the tables, and report the QC.
